Finibus allows you to take control over any territories on Earth and expand them freely.

Establish a territory over no-man's land
Collect natural resources, utilize them as factory inputs
Increase capability of empire by building more powerful buildings

Project Finibus was available in 2014

Project Finibus was developed and published to public players in the middle of 2014. The project itself was a prototype of an idea which has been later developed into "Planet of Cells" in early 2015.
"We would like to say thank you from our deepest mind to all players who participated in the project as beta players. Without you all, we would never have developed and refined our ideas into the next project."

Tao P. Ruangyam
Lead developer of StarColon Projects
